Daily market forecasts of volatility and direction

Our Technology

Finalysis provides what was previously thought impossible - daily market forecasts of volatility and direction inside a graph of market correlations. Developed by IBM Watson Alumni, this patent pending system uses Deep Learning and Multifractal Analysis to peek into the future with 85% accuracy.

Our Principles

"The most important thing is that you develop your own principles and ideally write them down, especially if you are working with others," proclaims billionaire investor Ray Dalio. At Finalysis, we did just that. Including:


  1. Honesty: Tell the truth, no matter what the consequences will be.
  2. Focus: Do and prioritize what is important now.
  3. Excellence: Deliver everything the best you can possibly make it.
  4. Growth: Improve upon yourself and your work each and every day.
  5. Perseverance: Continue until you reach the objective.
  6. Assistance: Ask for and give help to others. We rise or fall as a team
  7. Gratitude: Be thankful for your work, the team, and the opportunities.
  8. Learning: Make each and every work day a learning experience.
  9. Commitment: Deliver what you say by the required time
  10. Communication: Seek to understand and to be understood
